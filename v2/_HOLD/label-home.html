Deal pipeline

From intake to signed agreement

The label side is a **pipeline**, not chaos. You collect the right data once, let the contract template do the heavy lifting, and keep everything stored through your **local JSON engine + Netlify functions**.

Step 1 — Onboarding
Label / Artist intake form
Use label-onboarding.html to capture: label name, reps, artist names, project info, splits, territory, term & advance. This data is saved into localStorage for the next step.
Label & artist details Splits & term Territory & advance
Route: label-onboarding.html Feeds into: label-contract-lab.html
Step 2 — Contract Lab
Auto-built contract draft
label-contract-lab.html loads your onboarding data and generates a working contract draft you can fully edit before saving. When you click save, it hits label-contract-save and stores into your contracts JSON store.
Editable template Saved via Netlify function JSON-backed archive
Routes: label-contract-lab.html → /.netlify/functions/label-contract-save
Step 3 — Output
Printable / copyable contract
label-contract-output.html pulls a contract by ID from your contracts store via label-contract-get. You get a clean page to print, export as PDF, or copy text into your e-signature platform.
Print-ready Copy to clipboard Lookup via ID
Route: label-contract-output.html?id=CONTRACT_ID Function: /.netlify/functions/label-contract-get
Step 4 — Archive
Local JSON deal library
Every contract you save becomes part of your **internal library**. Later we can add filters, search, and summaries — but the foundation is already set with the contracts store.
JSON engine Versionable Search-ready
Stored in: contracts.json (via your Netlify JSON helper).
