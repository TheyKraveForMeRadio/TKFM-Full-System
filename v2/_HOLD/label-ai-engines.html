3. God View safety rails for AI

This section makes it clear that **no AI is free to go crazy**. God View sets **rails**. Every engine stays inside them.

Pricing rails
How far AI Autopilot Pricing is allowed to move a number without you touching anything.
  • Example: max ±20% without owner review.
  • Above that = AI can only suggest, not change live pricing.
  • Rules are set in God View, respected by App Hub + label packs.
Contract rails
What terms AI Contract Generator is **never** allowed to write.
  • Example: no term longer than 3 years without explicit override.
  • Certain clauses flagged as “owner must write manually”.
  • AI only assembles from templates you approve.
AI DJ & social rails
Where AI is allowed to touch **creative surfaces**: radio and social.
  • AI DJ Engine access always starts at **off** for every account.
  • AI social autopost can run in “draft only” mode forever.
  • Any “full autopilot” switches are owner-only, in God View.
God View page: god-view.html (owner-only rules). AI engines here are **label tools** that respect those rules.
